About Me

Hello and welcome! My name is Susi, and I am a passionate fullstack web developer with a background in visual art.

After spending the majority of my adult life in working as an artist, I decided to explore my love for designing and creating beautiful websites by teaching my self the basics of frontend web development. I wanted to take my skills to the next level, so I took the plunge and enrolled in the Ironhack web development bootcamp in Berlin, where I learned the ins and outs of web development and graduated as a fullstack developer.

My skills include HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Node.js and Express, as well as expierence in database management with MongoDB. But more than just technical skills, I have a passion for creating visually stunning websites that engage and captivate users. If you're looking for a skilled web developer who is passionate about creating beautiful, user-friendly websites, then look no further. Let's work together to bring your vision a life!
